What makes this different
The problem

Life doesn't come in
neat categories.

There are apps for your fitness. Apps for your finances. Apps for your sleep. Apps for your mental health. None of them talk to each other — and none of them see the full picture.

But your life isn't a collection of separate problems. It's one interconnected thing. And what's draining your energy might not be what you think it is.

  • 😶‍🌫️You're functioning fine on the outside but something feels off and you can't quite name it
  • 🔄You keep starting things with real enthusiasm — then losing momentum somewhere in the middle
  • 💸You're probably paying for three things you forgot about and overpaying on at least two more
  • 📋There's a mental to-do list that never gets done, quietly draining energy every single day
  • 🧭You know something needs to change. You're just not sure exactly what — or where to start
